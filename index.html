FAQ

Common Questions

What is AI automation for business?+
AI automation for business means using artificial intelligence and workflow tools (like N8N, Zapier, Make.com) to automatically handle tasks that humans currently do manually — responding to leads, booking appointments, updating CRMs, sorting emails, generating content, and more. The result: your team does less repetitive work and your business runs faster, with fewer errors.
What businesses benefit most from this?+
Any business that relies on responding to inbound leads, booking appointments, or managing customer relationships benefits enormously. This includes dental and medical clinics, service companies (HVAC, roofing, salons), marketing agencies, real estate agents, coaches, and e-commerce stores. If you're losing time on manual tasks that follow a pattern — I can automate it.
How long does setup take?+
Most systems are live within 1–2 weeks. Simple single-workflow projects can be delivered in 3–5 business days. Complex multi-channel systems with AI agents, CRM integration, and voice capabilities typically take 2–3 weeks. I always provide a clear scope and fixed timeline before any work begins.
Do I need technical knowledge to use this?+
Absolutely none. Every system I build comes with documentation, a full walkthrough, and ongoing support. You operate your business normally — the AI runs in the background handling the automations. If anything needs to change, you tell me and I handle it. Your job is just to review the results.
What channels does the AI agent support?+
The multi-channel messaging agent supports: Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, SMS, Email, and live website chat — all from a single AI agent. Voice AI can be added for inbound and outbound phone calls. Every channel feeds into the same CRM and booking system, so you always have a unified view of every lead.
What happens if the AI can't handle a request?+
Every system has human escalation pathways built in. When the AI detects a request outside its confidence threshold — a complex complaint, a high-value custom deal, a sensitive situation — it automatically flags for human review and routes to your team via Slack or email. Nothing important ever drops. Plus: error logging and Slack alerts so you always know what's happening in real time.
What does it cost?+
Projects are priced based on scope, complexity, and number of integrations. I always provide a clear, fixed quote before any work starts — no hourly billing surprises. Book a free 30-minute call and I'll give you an honest estimate within 24 hours. Most systems pay for themselves within the first month from recovered leads and time saved.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes — I work with clients globally. Based in Daet, Bicol, Philippines, I currently serve clients in the US, Australia, Canada, UK, and Southeast Asia. I'm available across time zones via WhatsApp, Calendly, email, or Messenger. Communication is always clear, responsive, and async-friendly for international collaboration.
